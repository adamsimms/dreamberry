# Code of Conduct

Dreamberry is a personal artwork. Contributors and discussants are expected to:

- Be respectful in issues, PRs, and related spaces.
- Stay within the artistic boundaries in [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) —
  especially: do not restore a live-camera fiction, and do not merge hold vs
  signal-lost aesthetics.
- Not harass, demean, or dox anyone.

Report concerns to **hello@adamsimms.xyz**. The artist may refuse, edit, or
remove contributions that violate these norms or the piece’s ethics.
